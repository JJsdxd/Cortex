#Academic/S2/IOT

The process of collecting, modelling and  processing data sets to 
	discover underlying patterns 
	or meaningful insights to allow for better decision making
**Collection and management** :- procuring data from various sources sensor nodes, user inputs and organizing it in forms that are suitable for further analysis

**TYPES**
	Descriptive describes the various happenings inferable from the data
		eg:- Sales reports
	Diagnostic :- discovering the **WHY** from the said data interlinking patterns between the variables etc..
		eg:- why sales dropped/increased : new ad campaign
	Predictive :- discovering repeating patterns/trends and applying them to future scenarios like increasing amount of stock of particular toy that sells more during winter than summer etc..
	prescriptive :- what changes to be made to have a desired outcome
		eg :- to increase overall sales increase production of items that usually sell out regularly

It can be done Graphically using [[IoT data visualisation tools]]
